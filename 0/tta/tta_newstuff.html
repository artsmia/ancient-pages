New at the Institute!

ArtReach Centers

This fall several of our curriculum materials will be available for you to preview and rent at the following sites in Minnesota: the Itasca Art Center, Grand Rapids; the Tweed Museum of Art, Duluth; and the Owatonna Art Association, Owatonna. The program is supported by a generous grant from the Blandin Foundation. Call Laura Pereira at (612) 870-3134 for more information.

Teacher Membership at the Institute

We're pleased to announce a new membership program for educators that will help you bring art into the classroom. It offers all the basic benefits of our Individual and Household memberships at a discount, plus special benefits just for teachers. This teacher membership program is supported by a generous grant from Ashland Inc.

Individual Educator Membership $25 (regularly $30)
Household Educator Membership $35 (regularly $40)
Basic benefits include:
  • Monthly Arts magazine
  • Free admission to films and special events. Program
  • Invitations to members openings
  • Special teacher-only event and communications
  • 10% museum shop discount
  • $10 gift certificate for Teaching the Arts products and services

Educator Plus Membership $50
Additional benefits include:
  • $20 gift certificate for Teaching the Arts products and services
  • Free copy of Selected Works: The Minneapolis Institute of Arts
  • Free tote bag (new members only)
Become a educator member today! Call the Institute's Membership Office at 870-3030 to join or for more information.
