Educational Services throughout Minnesota

Even if you can't make it to Minneapolis, you can still take advantage of the Institute's resources. In addition to sending materials by mail, we offer several other ways to access the museum and its collection. Call Laura Pereira at 870-3134 for more information about these services.

Curriculum Materials On-Line
A wealth of museum-related information and resources is now available on the World Wide Web, including an expanded version of this catalog and our World Mythology in Art curriculum packet.

More than 30 Art Education links can also be found on the Curriculum Materials page, and we hope to add even more resources throughout the year. Please leave your comments in the mailbox to let us know what you think.

ArtReach Centers
Many of our curriculum materials are available free at several sites in Minnesota:

  • The Itasca Art Association, Grand Rapids, (218) 326-2697
  • The Tweed Museum of Art, Duluth, (218) 726-8222
  • Owatonna Art Association, Owatonna, (507) 451-0533
  • New York Mills Regional Cultural Center, New York Mills, (218) 385-7609
