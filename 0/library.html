The Institute has a noncirculating research and reference library filled with resources on art history and the museum's permanent collection, including a full set of Institute publications.

WINTER HOURS:
The library is open Tuesday, Wednesday, Friday, and Saturday from noon until 5 p.m. and Thursday from noon until 8 p.m.

MEMORIAL DAY TO LABOR DAY:
Open Tuesday through Friday from noon until 5 p.m

The library is closed on Sundays and Mondays. For more information call (612) 870-3117
