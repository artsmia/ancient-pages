Directions to the Institute
5k
The Minneapolis Institute of Arts is located at 2400 Third Avenue South, one mile south of Downtown Minneapolis.

Coming From North and East on 35W
Take 94 West to the Lyndale exit. Go South on Lyndale to 24th Street. Turn left on 24th and proceed to Third Avenue South. Turn right on Third Avenue and proceed to 25th Street; the parking ramp is on the right and the Institute is located just North of the ramp.

Coming from West on I-394:
Take Hennepin/Lyndale exit off I-394. Go South on Lyndale to 24th Street. Turn left on 24th Street and proceed to Third Avenue South. Turn right on Third Avenue and proceed to 25th Street; the parking ramp is on the right and the Institute is located just north of the ramp.

Coming from St. Paul on I-94:
Take the 11th Street exit and curve left to Third Avenue South. Turn left on Third Avenue and proceed to 25th Street; the parking ramp is on the right and the Institute is located to the North of the ramp.

Coming from South on 35W:
Take 31st Street exit off 35W. Turn left on 31st and proceed to First Avenue South. Turn right and proceed to Third Avenue South. Turn right and proceed to 25th Street; the parking ramp is on the right and the Institute is located just North of the ramp.

Special needs?
The Institute is barrier-free and accessible to visitors with special needs. Wheelchairs are available inside the main entrance to the museum. There is reserved parking for handicapped visitors in the museum's parking ramp, south of the Third Avenue entrance. For more information, please call the Visitor Information Center at (612) 870-3131, TTD (612)-870-3132 or toll-free 800/876-ARTS (2787).
