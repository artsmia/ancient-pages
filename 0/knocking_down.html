Thanks to the generous support of our members, the museum is getting bigger and better than ever before!

Whenever you see this symbol, you'll also see signs of museum expansion.

In March, the Museum Shop is temporarily moved into the Cargill gallery on the main floor while the original Shop space undergoes an expansion that will double its size. Also this month, watch for significant movement of Asian and Greco-Roman objects as well as certain Decorative Arts galleries to prepare the second and third floors for construction. On April 8, the Art Research and Reference Library will close until further notice due to the construction project.

Please call the Visitor and Member Center at (612) 870-3131 or email miagen@artsMIA.org before you visit the museum to find out detailed information about which areas (and works of arts) are affected by construction activity. We apologize for any temporary inconvenience and we thank you for your support.
