Arty Party-Episode II

Wednesday, May 10, 2000
5:30-8:30 P.M.

The museum’s inter-active Art Cart allows visitors to touch authentic African artifacts and learn about African art from trained guides.

This free event celebrates the many businesses that help us to bring art to life for our community. The Arty Party is open to business supporters of the museum, who represent their company and its support of the museum’s mission. Invitations have been distributed to key individuals within each company; invitees are welcome to bring a business colleague.

Join us for music, drinks, hors d’oeuvres, and the most exciting exhibition this side of the millennium—the Smithsonian’s Star Wars: The Magic of Myth. Grab a cocktail, go on a scavenger hunt, or just simply stare at the wall—after all, we have nearly 100,000 works of art to admire.

But most of all, accept our appreciation for your generous corporate support of the museum

Following is a sampling of the special entertainment and activities planned for the evening:

  • Make your own nametag! (it is an art party, after all)
  • Hors d’oeuvres and cash bar and available throughout the evening
  • Remarks by Evan M. Maurer, Director and President at 6 P.M.
  • Complimentary exhibition admission to Star Wars: The Magic of Myth (audio tour available for $4)
  • Pose for a photograph with Darth Vader ($1)
  • Scavenger Hunt Through the Centuries
  • Music and Mingling
