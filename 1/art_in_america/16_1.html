About the Artist ~ Development of Minneapolis ~ About this Object ~ Style ~ Look Questions ~ Think Questions
Key Points

  • Robert Koehler's painting provides a PICTURESQUE view of everyday life in Minneapolis in the early 20th century.
  • The bustling thoroughfare, architecture, modes of transportation, and fashions all show the vital development of Minneapolis at this time.
  • With loosely applied BRUSHSTROKES and a limited PALETTE, Koehler captures the light and atmosphere of a rainy evening.
