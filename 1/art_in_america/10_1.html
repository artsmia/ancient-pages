Key Points

  • Jasper Cropsey used several COMPOSITIONAL devices and ATMOSPHERIC PERSPECTIVE to capture the expanse of the Catskill Mountains in this LANDSCAPE.
  • The forests of the Catskill Mountains awed and fascinated easterners who had not ventured west to the frontier.
  • Nature became a popular subject in America by the mid-19th century, largely because of the painters of the Hudson River School.
