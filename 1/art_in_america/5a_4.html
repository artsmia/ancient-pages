Decorative Motifs

A STYLIZED design of curving stems, leaves, and flowers decorates the lid of the caddy. On each FLUTE of the caddy is a drapery MOTIF, showing swags and tassels. Such motifs were popular in English NEOCLASSICAL designs. Silversmiths ENGRAVED the motifs into the silver with a sharp instrument.
