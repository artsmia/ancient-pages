Black Americans and Plantation Life ~ About the Artist ~ About this Object ~ The African House at Melrose Plantation ~ Look Questions ~ Think Questions
Key Points

  • Clementine Hunter painted the daily activities of black Americans on southern plantations during the mid-20th century.
  • The flat simple forms, bright colors, and COMPOSITION of The Wash are characteristic of Hunter's FOLK ART STYLE.
  • The Wash shows how laundry was done, what the workers wore, and the appearance of the African House at Melrose Plantation.
