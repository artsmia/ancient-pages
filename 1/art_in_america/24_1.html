Key Points

  • Alexander Calder invented the MOBILE, a form of sculpture that moves.
  • Air currents animate the many pieces of the large mobile Ahab, constantly changing its appearance.
  • The ORGANIC forms of Ahab evoke sealife and other aspects of the natural world.
