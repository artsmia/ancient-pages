Key Points

  • As an artist and an advocate of photography as a fine art, Alfred Stieglitz revolutionized modern photography.
  • Stieglitz considered The Steerage a perfect photograph because of the relationships of shapes in it and the deep human feelings it spontaneously captured.
  • The Steerage shows immigrants returning to their European homelands on the steerage level of a fashionable ship.
