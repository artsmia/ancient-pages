Key Points

  • Lake Superior and its surroundings have had a profound effect on the work of Minnesota artist George Morrison.
  • By attaching THREE-DIMENSIONAL shapes to a TWO-DIMENSIONAL surface, Morrison combines characteristics of both painting and sculpture in this COLLAGE.
  • The HORIZON LINE is a very important element of this ABSTRACT LANDSCAPE.
