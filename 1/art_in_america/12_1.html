Key Points

  • Cadzi Cody, a Shoshone Indian, painted images of several Plains Indian traditions on this elk hide, including the sacred sun dance, a wolf dance, and a bison hunt.
  • Cadzi Cody and other artists transformed traditional art forms into saleable items to earn income while confined to reservations.
  • The COMPOSITION, bright commercial colors, and curving CONTOUR lines all make this a dynamic painting.
