Look Questions Teacher Answer Key

1. Describe Abigail Gowen. How old does she look? What color is her hair? Her eyes? What does this PORTRAIT tell you about her personality?

2. What colors dominate this PORTRAIT? (Various grays.) Which color is the brightest? (Blue.)

3. Does this painting look flat or THREE-DIMENSIONAL? Which parts look the flattest? Why? Which parts look most three-dimensional? Why?
