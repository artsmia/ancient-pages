Charleston Drawing Room
Historic House ~ About this Object ~ Look Questions ~ Think Questions
Key Points

  • This PERIOD ROOM is the drawing room from a house built in 1772 in Charleston, South Carolina.
  • The architecture and furnishings of the room show the influence of English style and taste in the colonies.
  • The items in the room illustrate the leisure activities of a prosperous British family living in the South before the American Revolution.
