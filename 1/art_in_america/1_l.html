Look Questions

1. Is this artwork TWO-DIMENSIONAL or THREE-DIMENSIONAL? What clues led you to your answer? What is this object? How can you tell? Of what material is this bowl made? How can you tell?

2. In what animal form is the bowl made? What features of this animal did the artist include? What shapes did the artist use to represent each part of the bird?

3. Is this object NATURALISTIC or ABSTRACT?

4. SYMMETRICAL balance is an exact balanced arrangement of the same forms on both sides of an imaginary vertical line through the center of an object. ASYMMETRICAL balance is an arrangement of forms that do not appear the same on both sides of the imaginary line, yet are visually equal. Is this bowl symmetrical or asymmetrical? Explain your answer.
