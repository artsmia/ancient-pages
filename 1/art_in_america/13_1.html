Title
The Seneca Nation ~ The Tradition of Feasting ~ About this Object ~ Technique ~ Look Questions ~ Think Questions
Key Points

  • Seneca men carved special wooden ladles and other implements for use at feasts.
  • Feasts were a central part of an ancient tradition of thanksgiving for Woodlands peoples.
  • This ladle is carved in the form of a bird, an animal that had spiritual significance to the Seneca.
