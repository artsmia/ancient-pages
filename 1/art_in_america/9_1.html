St. Anthony Falls
St. Anthony Falls and the Development of Minneapolis ~ About the Artist ~ About this Object ~ Look Questions ~ Think Questions
Key Points

  • Although Henry Lewis's painting of St. Anthony Falls appears very NATURALISTIC, it does not present the commercial development that had already taken place at this site.
  • LANDSCAPE paintings like this one appealed to a public accustomed to urban industrialization and fascinated by the western wilderness during the expansion era.
  • The Mississippi River and the falls played a pivotal role in the exploration and settlement of the Minnesota territory and the economic development of the Twin Cities.
