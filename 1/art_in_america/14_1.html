Woman's Dress
The Lakota ~ The Turtle ~ Traditional Clothing ~ About this Object ~ Look Questions ~ Think Questions
Key Points

  • A Lakota woman made this dress from animal hide and embroidered it with glass beads.
  • Made for dancing on special occasions, this type of dress is called a "best dress."
  • The ABSTRACT design of the beaded yoke represents the sacred turtle by the lakeshore.
