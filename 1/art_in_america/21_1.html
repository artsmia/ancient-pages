Key Points

  • The social-documentary photographer Dorothea Lange used her art to change social conditions during the Great Depression.
  • Migrant Mother, a portrayal of a homeless working family, is an ICON of the Great Depression.
  • The power of this image relies not only on the woman's expression, but also on Dorothea Lange's point of view and COMPOSITION.
