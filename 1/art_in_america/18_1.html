Key Points

  • Georgia O'Keeffe was one of the earliest ABSTRACT painters in the United States.
  • In City Night O'Keeffe reduced skyscrapers to their most essential abstract elements - straight lines and broad surfaces.
  • The simplified forms of City Night represent O'Keeffe's impressions of New York City at night more than its exact appearance.
