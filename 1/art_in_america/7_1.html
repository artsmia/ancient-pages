View of Fort Snelling
Fort Snelling ~ About the Artist ~ About this Object ~ Look Questions ~ Think Questions
Key Points

  • Edward K. Thomas painted this bird's-eye view of Fort Snelling while he served at the fort as a sergeant in the U.S. Army.
  • Although the artist's lack of formal training is evident in his handling of space, the painting provides a detailed picture of the fort and its surroundings at this time.
  • Thomas's representation of the Dakota Indians is inaccurate and stereotypical.
