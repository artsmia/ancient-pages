Key Points

  • Herbert Hoover stressed his modest beginnings in a small Iowa town during his campaign to become the 31st president of the United States.
  • Grant Wood's painting of Hoover's birthplace is as much about American myth-making as about a historical site.
  • The LINEAR forms, decorative PATTERNS, and close attention to details in this painting are hallmarks of Wood's REGIONALIST style.
