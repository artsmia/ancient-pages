Key Points

  • Thomas Sully's full-length PORTRAIT of George Washington presents a heroic image of the president.
  • CLASSICAL images in this painting express the desire of the new nation's leadership to associate itself with the ideals of the Roman republican government.
  • The three-quarter pose, classical setting, and dramatic drapery are traits of formal portraiture in the European manner.
