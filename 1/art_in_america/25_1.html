Key Points

  • Grace Hartigan's early paintings bridge two American art movements - ABSTRACT EXPRESSIONISM and POP ART.
  • The colorful commercial images in Billboard reflect the boom in product advertising and consumerism in the United States after World War II.
  • The large size, visible BRUSHSTROKES, and all-over COMPOSITION of Billboard are all qualities of abstract expressionist painting.
