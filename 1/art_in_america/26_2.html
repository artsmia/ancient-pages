Bob Thompson

During the 1960s, Bob Thompson's brightly colored paintings earned him unprecedented success for a young black painter. Thompson grew up in Louisville, Kentucky, where he developed an enthusiasm for music, especially jazz. He also began his career as an artist there and formed a group called Gallery Enterprises, along with other black artists. In 1959, Thompson moved to New York City's Lower Eastside, where he became involved with a community of AVANT-GARDE musicians, visual artists, and poets.
