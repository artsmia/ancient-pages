Social Realism ~ Discrimination in the American Workforce~ About the Artist ~ About this Object ~ Look Questions ~ Think Questions
Key Points

  • Romare Bearden documented black American life and culture in his SOCIAL-REALIST art.
  • Fortune magazine COMMISSIONED Bearden to paint Factory Workers to illustrate an article on the importance of blacks in America's workforce during World War II.
  • The bold CONTOUR lines and exaggerated features in Factory Workers reveal Bearden's experience as a political cartoonist.
