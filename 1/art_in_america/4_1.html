About the Artist ~ The Subject ~ About this Object ~ Look Questions ~ Think Questions
Key Points

  • John Singleton Copley painted this PORTRAIT of a colonial American woman in the manner of English aristocratic portraits.
  • Although self-taught, Copley NATURALISTICALLY painted the facial features, surface TEXTURES, and reflection of light in this portrait.
  • Portraits were status symbols since only wealthy colonists could afford them.
