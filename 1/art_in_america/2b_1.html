Key Points

  • PERIOD ROOMS in museums are actual rooms or portions of rooms from historic houses.
  • This parlor served as both the main living room and the bedroom in a middle-class farmhouse built in Foxon, Connecticut, in 1740.
  • This room and its furnishings reflect the European heritage and tastes of the early Americans who lived there.
