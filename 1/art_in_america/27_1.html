Key Points

  • Chuck Close is considered a PHOTO-REALIST artist because he uses photographs as the source of his paintings.
  • Close's PORTRAIT of his friend Frank is more about how a photograph of Frank looks than about Frank's personality.
  • The huge SCALE, unflattering view, and blandness of Frank's expression are characteristic of Close's early paintings.
