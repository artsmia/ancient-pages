About the Artist ~ The Years After the Civil War ~ About this Object ~ Look Questions ~ Think Questions
Key Points

  • John F. Peto's STILL LIFE painting commemorates 1865, the final year of the Civil War and the year of Abraham Lincoln's assassination.
  • Using oil paint on canvas, Peto created the illusion of actual objects on a wooden door.
  • The images and somber colors reflect both America's and the artist's pessimism at the turn of the century.
