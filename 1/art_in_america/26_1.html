Key Points

  • Bob Thompson's painting Homage to Nina Simone celebrates the singer Nina Simone and expresses the artist's feelings about jazz music.
  • By carefully arranging the boldly colored figures into triangular groups, Thompson lends a sense of calm to this lively scene.
  • Thompson freely based the COMPOSITION of his homage on a 17th-century painting that he studied in Paris showing a celebration of Bacchus, the Roman wine god.
