Purcell-Cutts House

The Purcell-Cutts house is an excellent example of the Prairie School style of American architecture. The house is open to the public the second weekend of each month. Admission is $5 for adults; $4 for students and seniors; and free to Institute members and children 12 and under. Reservations are required; call (612) 870-3131.
