André Breton, in the Surrealist Manifesto: “I believe in the future transmutation of those two seemingly contradictory states, dream and reality, into a sort of absolute reality, of surreality, so to speak.” From Chipp, The Theories of Modern Art, 1968.

Q: How many Surrealists does it take to screw in a light bulb?
