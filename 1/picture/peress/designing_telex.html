Designing Telex Iran

Gilles Peress considered several possibilities for the cover of Telex Iran before finally settling on the picture of Pro-Shariatmadari Demonstration, Tabriz. A very early choice was simply a small reproduction of a photo cemented onto a five-by-seven-inch card.

A later version took a scrapbook approach

Peress finally settled on the Pro-Shariatmadari Demonstration, Tabriz picture and the title Telex Iran.
