Build an Exhibit allows you to create your own exhibit from the collections of The Minneapolis Institute of Arts and the Walker Art Center. To build an exhibit, you will select works of art, organize them according to an idea, and write your own labels. Each exhibit you build can be saved to review and revise later or share with others.
