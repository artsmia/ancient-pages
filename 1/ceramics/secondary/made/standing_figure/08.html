So, as an artist, I am thinking,

How did they make this figure anyway?

Like most artists, they used the materials and the technology that were available to them.

Of course, 2000 years ago there wasn't any electricity or running water, which made things more difficult than today.

Even though the standing figure was made so long ago, I can still tell, because I'm an expert potter, that it was made by a trained craftsperson.
