When you look at the top of the seed jar, you can see what looks like bird wings and feathers.

These are not REALISTICALLY painted. They are very SIMPLIFIED paintings of wings and feathers.

So, instead of seeing the details of a feather you see a GEOMETRIC SHAPE like a triangle or a rectangle.

This is the process of making something ABSTRACT.

It is similar to what some artists call "morphing"--when an object or person changes shape and becomes something else.
