A woman named Dextra Quotskuyva (coat- SKWEE-va) made this seed jar by hand out of clay. She belongs to a family of well-known Native American potters named NAMPEYO.

Dextra Quotskuyva lives among the PUEBLO people, who reside in many villages throughout New Mexico and Northern Arizona. She is a member of the Pueblo group called the HOPI-TEWA.
