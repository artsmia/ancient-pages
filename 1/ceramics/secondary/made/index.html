Hi, everyone! I am an artist. Artists have many talents.

As an artist, I always want to understand how art was made. What exactly did the artist have to do to make a work of art look the way it does?

I am a creative problem-solver. I have to think about many things before I actually begin.

I have to plan. I have to consider the look, materials, function, and cost of the piece before I create it.

Art making, to me, is more than simply making something. It is a spiritual process of self-discovery, creativity, and passion.

Follow me and discover some methods artists use to create their works of art.
