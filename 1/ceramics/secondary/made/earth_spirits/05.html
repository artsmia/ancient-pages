These creatures have a cat's body with the horns and hooves of a deer, a ferocious head, flaring wings, and flamelike projections. Both figures sit on pedestals. One beast has a human face with EXAGGERATED ears.

The human face was left unglazed and would have been painted after the piece was fired. Because the face wasn't GLAZED, the paint or pigment wore off over time.

The other creature has a colorful glazed cat face.
