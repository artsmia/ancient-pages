Detail of feathers

 

 

If you look at the top of the seed jar, you can see what looks like bird wings and feathers.

These are not realistically painted. They are very simplified paintings of wings and feathers.

So, instead of seeing the details of a feather you see a geometric shape like a triangle or a rectangle.

This is the process of making something abstract.

Some artists today call this "morphing." You know MORPHING, like the Ninja Turtles, Power Rangers, or Animorphs. You see that on TV or in movies all the time.
