This ceramic vessel is a seed jar that was made around the time the internet was started in the United States, in the early 1990s.

As a potter I can tell the seed jar is from the Nampeyo family because of the motif that is painted across the top of the flattened sphere.

Among the Pueblo peoples every family passes on designs that are like a signature for their family¹s pottery.
