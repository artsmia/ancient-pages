I notice, though, that the artist shaped the face REALISTICALLY. Why would Ife artists use IDEALIZATION alongside REALISM?

Well, one view is that idealization might have been used to show inner, invisible SPIRITUALITY.

And realism might have been used to show the outer, visible, physical self.

Works of art give us many clues about the people who made them if we look closely.
