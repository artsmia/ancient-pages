Look closely at the details on the shrine head: the lines on the face, the ridges for the hair. The people of Ife used hairstyles and body decorations to identify themselves, much as we do today. This hair style shows the woman's high status among the Yoruba.

The VERTICAL LINES in her face form a pattern. They show the practice of SCARIFICATION in Ife. Scarification is similar to tattooing or body piercing in that it identifies people as part of a group.
