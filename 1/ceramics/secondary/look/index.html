Greetings! I am an art historian, and I work as a curator in a museum like The Minneapolis Institute of Arts.

As an art historian and museum curator, I am responsible for gathering historical information about each work of art in the collection.

I also examine each piece of art very closely, so I know exactly what it looks like.

Like a scientist or a detective, I am skilled at making careful observations. I connect what I see with what I have learned from books and documents.

I believe every work of art has a story to tell, and I want to know that story.

Come with me on a short journey to discover what we can learn about history by looking at works of art.
