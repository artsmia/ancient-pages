A lot of changes were going on in the world in the late 1800s. Because businesses were able to produce more products, the COMPETITION between them became greater.

To get more customers, businesses started using POSTERS to ADVERTISE their products to the public.

Remember, there was no TV or radio then to run ads, so the poster became a common sight on the street. This turned into a new business, as companies began to rent walls or the sides of buildings, so they could put up their advertising.
