People still put up posters today, musicians to advertise their concerts, or the neighbor lady to advertise her garage sale. The BILLBOARD is really a very large advertising poster. You've seen billboards, haven't you?

Can you imagine our world without advertisements? We now wear advertisements on our clothes. Think about all the things you wear that may say Nike, Guess, the Grateful Dead, or Disney.

Did you ever think of yourself as a walking advertisement for businesses?
