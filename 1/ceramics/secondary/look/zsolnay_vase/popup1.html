Can you think of any ways that art and science are similar, or of ways that science is used in art?

Scientists need to make careful observations in their work, and so do artists and art historians. For example, in drawing a STILL LIFE, an artist must study lines and shapes closely in order to draw objects REALISTICALLY.

Artists use science when they mix colors. And artists need to know about the chemical makeup of materials they work with. For example, sculptors must understand how to bond materials together so they do not fall apart under pressure, and painters should know whether their paints and solvents are safe or need to be handled with caution.
