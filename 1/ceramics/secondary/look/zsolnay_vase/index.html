Hungary, a country of many STYLES.

Throughout history, many countries of Europe and Asia have wanted control of this section of the world. The Turkish, the Romans, Austria, Germany, Mongolia, and the Russians have all staked their claim to this area of the world.

To visit Hungary, you can see with your own eyes the influence these different countries have had.

Here you can find Roman architecture, a Far Eastern influence in ceramic vases, and German MOTIFS used as DECORATION.
