First of all, this vase can be cut in two so that both halves are the same shape.

They would be mirror images of each other. This means that the vase is SYMMETRICAL, and symmetry helps to give balance.

Then too, the size of the painted images is in PROPORTION to the part of the vase they are painted on. The largest image is on the largest part, and so on. This contributes to balance and to harmony and order also.
