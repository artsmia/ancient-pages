We call this an inkstone. It was found in a tomb in China. The ancient Chinese furnished tombs with various ceramic objects to accompany the dead into the afterlife.

The objects placed in the tombs were called ming chi. They were meant to accompany and protect the dead in the afterlife.

This inkstone, in the shape of a tortoise, identifies the person who was buried here as a Chinese scholar. He would have used it as one of his tools for writing.
