Look closely at the shell of the tortoise.

Notice the LINES INCISED on the shell's SURFACE.

The lines form shapes called HEXAGONS.

Remember, I mentioned that to the Chinese the tortoise was a SYMBOL of long life, strength, and endurance.

Well, the HORIZONTAL LINES you see inside the hexagons are symbols also.

They are called ba gua, or TRIGRAMS.

The trigrams can be combined to form 64 HEXAGRAMS.

If you wish to research these further, you need to look for the Chinese book of wisdom called the I Ching (Book of Change).
