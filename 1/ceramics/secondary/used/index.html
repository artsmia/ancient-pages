Hello, friends! I am an archaeologist. I search for clues to how people lived, by examining and piecing together artifacts from the past.

When I say artifacts, I am talking about objects that people have made.

I want to determine how those objects were used and what purposes they served.

I look at how tools and art objects reflect the culture that made them.

What is a culture, you ask?

Well, a culture is a group of people who share many of the same beliefs and values.

Try this: Look at dishes you have around your own house, like pots, bowls, coffee cups, or pitchers.

What would these objects tell a stranger about you and your family?

There are more clues than you may be aware of! Come with me to explore different cultures by examining the objects they left behind.
