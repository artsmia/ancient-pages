Use the World of Art to Learn About History and Cultures.

World Ceramics examines twelve objects from the collection of the Minneapolis Institute of Arts.

For information about how to use these activites in your classroom, please see our note to teachers.

Student Activities  

For Grade Levels K-5
Read and look at works of art to answer questions
(Modeled after the I Spy books)

For Grade Levels 6-9 Listen to three art professionals tell you how they examine works of art and the kinds of information they can uncover

For Grade Levels 10-12 Find out where ceramic pieces came from and how ceramics fit into different cultures at different times in history

For all Grade Levels

Compare ceramic objects to a basketball, or to each other

Learn how ceramic objects were made by following step by step descriptions

 

Listen to illustrated stories based on myths or folktales from each ceramic piece's culture

To download or view you must have the RealAudio player available from www.real.com
