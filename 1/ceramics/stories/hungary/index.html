Set in the 1920's, this story is adapted from the Hungarian book, "Zsolnay", in which Terez Zsolnay Mattyasovszky wrote about her childhood. Terez worked in her family's ceramic factory during the years that her brother ran it. Written by Jennifer Lack, the story is about Terez, as a grandmother, as she sits with her granddaughter and reads a diary entry she wrote during her childhood. The diary entry is fictitious, but uses some descriptions and incidents from Terez's writings. Read by Katherine Lack.
