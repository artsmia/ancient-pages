This story takes place during the Han Dynasty. It is based on Chinese folktales about the spirits of a tortoise and a snake. Written by Carol Brash, the story is a fictitious account of a little girl's journey as she tries to save her village from a drought. Read by Rose Chu. (Story length is 7 minutes 34 seconds)
