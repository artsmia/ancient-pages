This Hopi creation story, originally passed down for generations through oral tradition, was taken from, Pueblos Story Teller, by Diane Hoyt-Goldsmith. Like many traditional stories, this one plays an important role in the lives of the Hopi people. The Hopi are a Pueblo group that live in the Southwest part of the United States. The voice is that of Carley Bordeau.
