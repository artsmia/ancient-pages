Barber's Bowl

3-1/2 inches high, 10-1/4 inches in diameter
Gift of Mr. and Mrs. George R. Steiner
96.36.6

This fictional story incorporates historical details of a barber's tasks, which often included surgery, blood-letting and tooth-pulling, along with shaving and cutting hair. Written by Jennifer Lack, it tells of the experience of a young boy and girl who hide in the attic from their mom to avoid a trip to the dentist. Read by Patricia Kenton.
