Open House for Corporate Employees

All employees of corporate donors are invited to come with their families!

Each year The Minneapolis Institute of Arts salutes its supporters in the business community by hosting a free event for employees and their families. This year, the Corporate Open House will be Sunday, November 14, from noon until 5 p.m. The event has been planned in conjunction with “Celebrating Africa,” a special Family Day, recognizing the exhibitions “CHOKWE! Art and Initiation Among Chokwe and Related Peoples” and “Spirits Embodied: Art of the Congo.” This event has been tremendously popular in the past and many attendees tell us that it has become a tradition within their company.

The museum’s inter-active Art Cart allows visitors to touch authentic African artifacts and learn about African art from trained guides.

Join us November 14th, for music, munchies and mask-making! Following is a sampling of the special entertainment and activities planned for the afternoon:

  • Songs, music and dances of various ethnic groups of the Congo region.
  • Mask-making art activity for all ages.
  • Workshops in African dance, drumming, song and language.
  • Refreshments in our Corporate Lounge.

This open house provides a unique opportunity to recognize and celebrate the generosity of the Institute’s corporate supporters. Corporate employees will receive special gifts and privileges throughout the museum, including a one-time 10% discount at the Museum Shop.

We hope to see you there!
