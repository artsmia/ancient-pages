Art is for Everyone

Your experience of our permanent collection and special exhibitions can go beyond viewing the art in our galleries - it can be interactive. We encourage everyone to participate in our hands-on classes and workshops, attend lectures and films and join in docent-led tours. The Minneapolis Institute of Arts is your museum!

Hours: 12-5 p.m. Sunday; 10 a.m.-5 p.m. Tuesday, Wednesday, Friday, Saturday; 10 a.m.-9 p.m. Thursday. The Institute is closed Mondays, Thanksgiving, Christmas, and July 4.

Visitor Information: call toll-free (888) MIA-ARTS (642-2787), Local (612) 870-3131, TTD (612) 870-3132, e-mail us at wlee@artsmia.org
