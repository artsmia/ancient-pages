A Museum's Role as Conservator
One of the primary functions of museums, like The Minneapolis Institute of Arts, is to preserve great works of art so that they may endure the test of time and be experienced by many generations of visitors.

The "Restoration of a Masterwork" Exhibition
This special exhibition running from September 14th through October 24th is a unique opportunity to get an up-close look at the delicate process of restoring a work of art that is over 300 years old.

Upper Midwest Conservation Association
The restoration of this masterwork is being performed by members of the Upper Midwest Conservation Association.
