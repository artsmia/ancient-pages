25. In the last 350 years, the Castiglione has been restored two or three times, perhaps more. There are many holes and tears in the canvas, and repairs were probably made as damage occurred. The canvas was most probably lined in the 19th century and the present lining dates from the middle of the 20th century. There are several campaigns of retouching and overpaint on the painting, which indicates that more than one restoration took place.
