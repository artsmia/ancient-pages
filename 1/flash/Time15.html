Castiglione in Rome and Naples (1630s)

Castiglione moves to Rome, and in 1633 paints his first known signed and dated work, entitled Jacob's Journey. In 1634 he becomes a member of The Academy of St. Luke. In 1635 he goes to Naples for a few years, perhaps to meet with Jusepe de Ribera, an artist whom Castiglione greatly admired.
