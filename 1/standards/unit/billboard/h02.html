High School Level: Grades 9-12

High School Standard: Academic Writing

Create a commercial that is based on Billboard and is intended to persuade people to visit New York City. (See Activities, Language Arts.)

Write a poem using words that capture the essence of colors and images selected from a popular magazine aimed at a specific audience (e.g., Gourmet, Sports Illustrated). (See Activities, Language Arts.)

Discuss why Billboard can be considered a visual catalogue of New York City, read poems that use catalogues as a literary device, and write a poem based on a catalogue of words that express personal feelings about the local community. (See Activities, Language Arts.)

Research a particular place and time, such as New York City in the 1950s, and based on the findings, write a catalogue poem. Compare and contrast this catalogue poem with a personal catalogue poem (see preceding activity). (See Activities, Language Arts.)

High School Standard: Interpersonal Communication

As a member of a small group, assist in planning and constructing a collage that expresses the mood of the local community. Display and explain the collage to the class. (See Activities, Art.)

As a member of a small group, assist in creating a musical composition based on Billboard. Present the composition to the class. (See Activities, Music.)
