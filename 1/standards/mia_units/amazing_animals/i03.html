Intermediate Level: Grades 4-5

Intermediate Standard: Artistic Creativity, Performance, and Expression

View and discuss the unit's eight images, which represent the media of printmaking, ceramics, oil painting, bronze casting, and photography.

Use arts terminology to describe visual elements of artworks, express ideas about artworks, and identify styles of art belonging to different cultures and times.

Create artistic works in various media that relate to the unit's specific visual images as well as to the unit's theme. (See Amazing Animals Activities, Art.)

Do movement and drama activities related to specific visual images within the unit as well as to the unit's theme. (See Amazing Animals Activities, Movement and Drama.)

Do musical activities related to specific visual images within the unit as well as to the unit's theme. (See Amazing Animals Activities, Music.)
