High School Level: Grades 9-12

High School Standard: Themes of United States History

Gather information about a major event or period in U.S. history (e.g., Civil War, Great Depression, the 1950s), and then create a collage that captures the essence that event or period. (See Activities, Social Studies.)

Gather information about an American who has made an historically significant contribution (e.g., Thomas Jefferson, Susan B. Anthony, Frederick Douglass), and then create a collage that captures the essence of that person's life. (See Activities, Social Studies.)
