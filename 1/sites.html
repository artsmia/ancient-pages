Exemplary Museum Web sites

This list of links was compiled from the Interactive Media Groups web research. A list of over sixty sites were divided among the team members. Sites were rated in the categories below. In a final summary meeting, the sites listed below were mentioned as some of the best examples of this kind of stuff on the web.

This document is available at http://www.artsmia.org/sites.html
