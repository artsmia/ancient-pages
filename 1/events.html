Art is for Everyone

Your experience of the museum’s permanent collection and special exhibitions can go beyond viewing art in the galleries – it can be interactive! Participate in hands–on classes and workshops, attend lectures and films and join in docent–led tours. The Minneapolis Institute of Arts is your museum!
