Transforming the Body: Photography and the Nude

Part personal expression, part artistic invention, part social comment, and endlessly suspended between the erotic and the aesthetic, the nude has always been photography’s least understood genre. Expanding on the exhibition "Body Work: Photographs of Nudes," this class will examine the ways photographers have transformed the bodies in front of their cameras into powerful visual statements. In addition, participants will trace the relationship between the photographic nude and changing social conventions. Instructor: Vince Leo
