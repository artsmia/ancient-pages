World Mythology Storytellers

The following storytellers generously donated their time and talent to produce the audio cassette tape that accompanies the World Mythology in Art slide set. Many of them are available, for a nominal fee, to do artist-in-residence and other school programs. Feel free to contact the storytellers whose phone numbers appear with the credits below.
