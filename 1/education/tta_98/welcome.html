Dear Educator,

Come celebrate the world of art at the museum, or bring this enriching experience into your classroom with our curriculum materials and services. Our newly expanded galleries provide you with wonderful works of art to integrate into your lesson plans. We have thousands of new artworks from places near and far, from the United States and France to China, Japan, and Nigeria.

This catalog describes the museum's many resources and customized services that introduce students to the diverse world of art. Our classroom materials and guided tours are broadly based in the humanities and relevant to every area of study—from social studies and language arts to math and science. In addition, our educational resources can help your students achieve the new Minnesota Profile of Learning Preparatory and High Standards. This catalog includes at-a-glance matrices that link our tours and curriculum materials with specific learning areas and standards. Also be sure to visit our site on the World Wide Web at www.artsMIA.org. There, you can find a number of our materials on-line or explore our new World Ceramics unit for elementary and secondary students.

Welcome to our new museum! We're eager to introduce you and your students to our expanded galleries and resources.

The Education Division
The Minneapolis Institute of Arts
