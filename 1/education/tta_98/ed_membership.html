Educator Membership at the Institute

The membership program for educators helps you bring art into the classroom. It offers all the basic benefits of our individual and household memberships at a discount, plus special benefits just for teachers.

Individual Educator Membership $30 (regularly $35)
Dual Educator Membership $45 (regularly $50)

Basic benefits include:

  • Monthly Arts magazine
  • Free admission to films and special exhibitions
  • Invitations to members' openings
  • Special educators' event and communications
  • 10% Museum Shop discount
  • $10 gift certificate for Teaching the Arts products and services

Educator Plus Membership $60

Additional benefits include:

  • $20 gift certificate for Teaching the Arts products and services
  • Free poster from the Institute's new permanent collection series
