Three Bears' House

The Three Bears House, a beautiful Minnesota pine bear house built by St. Paul toymaker, Tom Fletcher, is on display in conjunction with 100 Years of Huggable Toys: The Gund Collection (November 7 - January 3). The beautifully crafted two- level cottage, which will go to a lucky raffle winner, is complete with cuddly Gund bears - Mama, Papa and Baby!

The play house is four feet wide, four feet tall and two feet deep, with an open back that provides easy access for playing. The exterior is trimmed in hand- carved Honduras mahogany with a dormered, birch bark roof and a cupola topped by green, leaf-shaped tiles. The upstairs floors are bare wood; the kitchen floors are wood that has been finished to resemble terra cotta tiles. The furnishings include three chairs, bowls and beds plus one table - everything needed for your bears to live comfortably! This durable house is meant for touch, not just looking, and makes a wonderful treasure to pass through generation after generation.
