Why Sponsorship?

Because it works. Several recent studies have shown that consumers understand and appreciate sponsorship—and that sponsorship can greatly influence buying decisions. In fact, sponsorship has the greatest impact on highly educated, higher income individuals who are difficult to reach through traditional media. Sponsorship allows a company to:

  • reinforce current marketing goals
  • bolster public relations activities
  • strengthen client relationships
  • reward high-performing employees
  • target its messages and audiences
  • distinguish itself from competitors


Picture your company associated with some of the world's greatest artists—Picasso, Monet, and Rembrandt.

Now picture becoming a corporate sponsor at The Minneapolis Institute of Arts, where you can achieve a prominence and make an impact available in few other media. In an environment where the medium is the message, a sponsorship relationship with The Minneapolis Institute of Arts represents an invaluable association with quality—and a promotional platform for reaching an upscale audience of nearly 600,000 per year.


Picture your company forging a direct link with a large and loyal audience

What's more, this large audience clearly understands and appreciates the companies that support the Institute's many outstanding programs. In a 1995 survey, 95% of Institute visitors had a positive image of a business that sponsors programs at the museum, and 39% said they would be more likely to purchase the products or services of a business participating in an Institute sponsorship.


Picture your company achieving multiple goals from a custom-designed sponsorship program

Corporate sponsorships at the Institute are specially tailored to the needs and nature of each sponsor's business. Past sponsors have leveraged their association to achieve employee-relations objectives, to entertain important clients, or to reward staff performance. All sponsorships include prominent recognition in the museum and in promotional materials, cross-promotional opportunities and options for corporate entertainment.
