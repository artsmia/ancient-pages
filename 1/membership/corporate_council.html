Corporate Council ...a lasting investment in the community

Summary of Annual Benefits

Members $1,500

  • Recognition in the museum’s Annual Report, Corporate Council brochure, and prestigious lobby panel
  • Subscription to Arts magazine
  • Invitations to exclusive events for designated company executive(s)
  • Invitations to annual Corporate Open House welcome event for all employees and their families
  • Discounts for employees on Institute memberships
  • Discounts for employees on group tickets to major exhibitions, such as "American Impressionism" and "Degas & America."

Benefactors $3,000

All of the above, plus:

  • A one-year Executive Membership for a designated employee, which includes benefits of membership and exhibition previews
  • Priority ticket notification, an opportunity for employees to purchase tickets for popular special exhibitions before they go on sale to the public

Partners $6,000

All of the above, plus:

  • Recognition in local business publications and Arts magazine

Associates $10,000

All of the above, plus:

  • Curators available to consult on your art acquisitions

Directors $25,000

All of the above, plus:

  • Opportunity to host a Corporate Day at the museum, a private event that combines gallery tours with entertainment for employees and their families

Sponsors $50,000

All of the above, plus:

  • Opportunity to sponsor a special museum program or exhibition

Because the estimated fair market value of these benefits is not substantial, your gift is fully tax-deductible.
