Member Travel

"Our tour was beautifully planned and executed— I loved it."

For over 20 years, The Minneapolis Institute of Arts' Members' Travel Program has dedicated itself to offering innovative tours—bringing the best of the world's art and architecture to our members. Our travel program strives to provide unique, up-to-the- minute experiences that far exceed general travel expectations—from world renowned museums and historic sites to impressive private collections. Expert guides or curators lead the tours.

For more information, to request itineraries or to make reservations, call Diane Hilliard, Coordinator of The Minneapolis Institute of Arts Members' Travel Program, at Hilliard and Olander, (612) 333-1440 or 1 800 229-8407.
