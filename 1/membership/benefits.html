A Museum for Members

The membership program helps you get the best possible museum experience! Browse through the membership categories below and find out more about the many benefits of membership.

Members

Valuable benefits and privileges help members save money, stay informed and increase their enjoyment of everything that goes on at the museum.

Contributors' Circle

Contributors' Circle level benefits are designed to expand our members' involvement in the many social, educational, and cultural opportunities offered by the museum.

Patrons' Circle

Patrons' Circle members enjoy exclusive benefits and privileges offered in appreciation of an annual commitment of $1,500 or more.

Educator Memberships

This special program for educators helps bring art into the classroom. It offers all the regular benefits of our Individual and Dual memberships, plus a special discount and added benefits just for teachers.

Corporate Council

The Institute's tradition of partnership and service continues today through the Corporate Council. While Corporate Council members receive a number of exclusive privileges, our statewide community receives the greatest benefit: a world-class museum accessible to all.

Business League

When you join the Business League, you help the Institute provide quality programs for all visitors. Your support is critical, and the rewards are many—for your company, for our community, and for generations to come.

New Century Society

The New Century Society recognizes those who have chosen to support the Institute through their wills, estate plans, or life income arrangements. Contributions received as a result of charitable planning help build for the future by supporting the programs and activities of the Institute in perpetuity.

Gift Membership

Giving an arts membership at the Institute for family, friends, and colleagues is a unique way to support an essential community resource and give a timeless, thoughtful, convenient gift for any occasion.
