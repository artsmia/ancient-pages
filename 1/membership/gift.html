The Gift of Membership makes a Thoughtful Present

When you buy a Minneapolis Institute of Arts Gift Membership, you give a full year of exciting membership benefits to a friend, colleague or loved one and support a vital community resource. As you know, members receive a subscription to Arts magazine, invitations to special events and preview parties, and discounts on many museum programs. Stop at the Visitor and Member Center desk to order a gift membership, or to charge your order by phone, please call the Membership Sales Office at (612) 870-6323. Please order early to ensure receipt before Christmas.

Gift Memberships are available at the following levels:

Individual $35
Dual $50
Family $65

Arts Circle Memberships

Individual $65

Family $95
