Arts Circle Membership

A membership for young professionals

Become part of the "next generation" of members that carries on the volunteer tradition of the Friends of the Institute. Arts Circle members receive all the benefits of general membership, plus Arts Circle extras (lectures, tours, social gatherings, and family events). These events are specially planned to fit into the busy career and family schedules of young professionals.

Arts Circle is a premium membership available at the following levels:

Individual $65
Dual $80
Family $95

Arts Circle membership and benefits are available at no extra charge to members joining at the Ambassador level ($125) or above. Please notify us if you would like to receive these special benefits.

(Two-year memberships not available for Arts Circle.)
