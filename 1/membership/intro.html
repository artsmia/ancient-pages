Bringing Art To Life For Everyone!

The Minneapolis Institute of Arts is a leading resource for the visual arts and a center for cultural activity throughout the community and the Upper Midwest. The museum offers something for everyone—from its diverse permanent collection to exciting special exhibitions to innovative arts education programs and cutting edge technology. Thanks to support from individual and corporate members, the Institute annually serves nearly 700,000 adults and young people.
