Every year about this time, a flower lover's fancy turns to thoughts of Art in Bloom at the Minneapolis Institute of Arts. Each spring, the galleries burst into bloom. More than 100 area garden club members create floral arrangements inspired by the works of are in the museum's collection. Professional floral designers enhance the lobby, courts, and other spaces with flowers and other green and growing things. Docents escort free tours of Art in Bloom.
