The Minneapolis Institute of Arts Museum Shop is a full-service art bookstore. We will ship anywhere.

MuseumShop.com
MuseumShop.com, also known as MuseumShop@Home, was created in 1997 by a consortium of museums to offer online visitors the largest selection of their products on the Web, and to publicize their exhibits and programs. Participating museums' objectives are to inspire culturally curious shoppers and allow visitors to find unique gifts and affordable treasures from a wide range of museums. Each museum features products from its own museum store, and enables visitors to shop either by museum or by product category. Each purchase helps to support the Museum Partners' ongoing programs.
