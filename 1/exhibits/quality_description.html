Quality Control: Ruthann Godollei, Alexa Horochowski, Lee Anne Swanson, and Carolyn Swiszcz.

Through June 18
Minnesota Artists Gallery
Artists gallery talk and mandala erasing ceremony: Sunday, June 18 at 3 p.m.

Paintings, prints, mixed media and installation work by four Minnesota artists are featured in this exhibition. These artists are known for their innovative use of materials and narrative irony: Ruthann Godollei decodes patterns of family and social dysfunction in sparse, scratchboard drawings and monoprints. Alexa Horochowski scrutinizes the institution of marriage in a multi-sensory laundry room equipped with bubbles, a working wringer washer and clothesline. Lee Anne Swanson combines a huge, moody painting with a line of waist-high skirted tanks containing glitter-covered mechanical duck decoys. Carolyn Swiszcz reveals her love for accidental gesture and flexible materials in a giant eraserboard mandala.
