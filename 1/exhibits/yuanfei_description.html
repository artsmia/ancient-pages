Melba Price
Land Locked, 1998

Yuanfei Hotel

October 1 - November 28, 1999
Minnesota Artists Gallery

Yuanfei Hotel features a new body of work by artist Melba Price, well known for her somber figurative drawings of women. For her MAEP installation, Price will appropriate motifs from 16th century Chinese landscape painting in a monochromatic, floor-to-ceiling treatment of the gallery walls. Her vision pays homage to Chinese art and culture and serves as a backdrop to a series of framed drawings, depicting ghostly women of varying and indeterminate eras.
