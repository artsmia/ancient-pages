Lecture: Ralph Rapson with Christopher Monkhouse & Thomas R. Fisher
Sunday, March 28, 1999, 2 p.m.
Pillsbury Auditorium

Join architect Ralph Rapson, Christopher Monkhouse, Bell Memorial Curator of Decorative Arts and Sculpture and Curator of Architecture at the MIA, and Thomas R. Fisher, Dean of the College of Architecture and Landscape Architecture at the University of Minnesota, for an afternoon of reminiscences and conversation about the pioneering contribution Rapson has made in the field of modern architecture and design.

 

Lecture: Jane Loeffler on Ralph Rapson
Thursday, April 15, 7 p.m.
Pillsbury Auditorium, free

Architectural historian Jane Loeffler will discuss Rapson’s designs, which projected America’s image as a free and open society with dramatic, modern and often controversial buildings.

Adult Class: Ralph Rapson: Design
Saturday, April 24

This class presents an overview of Rapson’s career and his influence on modernism, highlighting his residential design projects such as the University of Minnesota faculty housing in University Grove and the acclaimed Philip W. Pillsbury residence. It also includes a discussion of his design contributions to modern furniture and the decorative arts.
