Tickets go on sale Monday, January 10, for members and groups and Sunday, February 6, for nonmembers.

Tickets are issued for admission times every 30 minutes on the hour and half hour. The last timed tickets of the day will be issued one hour prior to museum closing.

Members

Members can call the Members Ticket Hotline (612-870-6323) or come to the museum to order their complimentary tickets and to purchase additional tickets. Your level of membership determines your number of free tickets:
 

Individual membership:

One free adult admission

Dual membership:

Two free adult admissions

Family membership:

Two free adult admissions and one for each registered child under 18

Ambassador through Sustaining:

Four free adult admissions
  
Groups 

Groups can call the Hotline (612-870-6323) or come to the museum to order their tickets.

Groups must have 15 or more people attending at the same time to be eligible for a group discount.

A group may be made up of both adults and children. Tickets for the adults, students and seniors in the group are $8; tickets for children ages 6-12 are $5.

School groups will be scheduled Tuesday through Friday for the 9, 9:30, 10 or 10:30 a.m. admission times only. The first three chaperones receive free tickets; additional chaperones pay $3 each.

Payment: Groups must pay half the total in advance to hold their tickets. The rest of the payment is due 30 days before the date of their tickets. A $3 handling fee is added to each order.

 

Nonmembers

Nonmembers can either come to the museum or call the Ticketmaster Artsline (612-673-0404) to order tickets. Tickets also available online through TicketMaster's Web site at www.ticketmaster.com
