Arnold Newman Portraits

March 4–July 23, 2000
Harrison Photography Gallery

Arnold Newman is one of the best-known and most distinguished portrait photographers of the 20th century. He pioneered “environmental portraiture,” a style that places subjects in carefully composed settings that capture their work and personality. Since the 1930s, Newman has used his impeccable technique and innovative strategies to photograph many of the most accomplished people of the century – artists, writers, actors, composers, politicians and others– for publications such as Life, Holiday, Fortune and The New Yorker.

Exhibition sponsored by the law firm of Fredrikson & Byron.

Arnold Newman’s appearance in Minneapolis has been generously supported by Canon U.S.A.
