A Lesson of Love
Treasuring Freedom
Robert O. Fisch

The Metamorphosis to Freedom

Now through November 19, 2000
Harold and Mickey Smith Gallery of Jewish Arts and Culture (362)

This installation of fourteen paintings and prose by Dr. Robert O. Fisch is a narrative of his personal journey from suppression to freedom and underscores the importance and privilege of being free.
