Theme

Combining scientific investigation with artistic concerns, Merian conveys with extraordinary delicacy the nature and beauty of a moth in various stages of development, as well as the plants upon which the insect thrived. Her published illustrations&emdash; of which this is an example&emdash;were based on careful studies of nature and provided new information to the sciences of botany and zoology.
