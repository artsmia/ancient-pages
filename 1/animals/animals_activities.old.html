Amazing Animals in Art: Suggested Activities

Grades K-6

Discussion of the artworks in the "Amazing Animals in Art" unit can lead to study across disciplines. The following art and interdisciplinary activities can be used to integrate the "Amazing Animals" images and themes into the elementary classroom curriculum.
