Educator Membership at the Institute

The membership program for educators helps you bring art into the classroom. It offers all the basic benefits of our individual and household memberships at a discount, plus special benefits just for teachers. This educator membership program is supported by a generous grant from Ashland Inc.

Individual Educator Membership $25 (regularly $30)
Household Educator Membership $35 (regularly $40)

Basic benefits include:

  • Monthly Arts magazine
  • Free admission to films and special exhibitions
  • Invitations to members' openings
  • Special teacher-only event and communications
  • 10% discount at Museum Shop
  • $10 gift certificate for Teaching the Arts products and services


Educator Plus Membership $50

Additional benefits include:

  • $20 gift certificate for Teaching the Arts products and services
  • Free copy of Selected Works: The Minneapolis Institute of Arts
  • Free tote bag (new members only)

Become an educator member today! Call the Institute's Membership Office at 870-3030 to join or for more information.
