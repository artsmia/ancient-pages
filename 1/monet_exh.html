Special Information

Monet at Vétheuil: The Turning Point
Showing May 31 through July 26
in the Dayton Hudson Gallery on the Institute's second floor

"Monet at Vétheuil: The Turning Point" is a focused exhibition of paintings tracing a defining moment in Monet's career. During his winter in the rural town of Vétheuil, Monet began to perfect the concepts and techniques seen in his later serial paintings-the works that established him as a pioneer in modern art. Don't miss this incredible opportunity to experience the 18 masterworks that launched Monet's rise to greatness.
