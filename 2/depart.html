The Department

The Minneapolis Institute of Arts' Media Department was formed in the mid-1970s. In 1991 the department was restructured and renamed the "Interactive Media Group" in order to better address the emerging trends in technology and to fulfill the Institute's goal of increasing visitor effectiveness through the use of multimedia. Since that time the Institutes' eight person Interactive Media Group has become an international leader in the development of museum media applications. This section provides an overview of the team members and professional accomplishments including access to selected papers and presentations.
