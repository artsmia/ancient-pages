ArtsConnectEd
www.artsconnected.org

This new Web site provides public access to the rich collections, references, archives, media, and curricula of both The Minneapolis Institute of Arts and the Walker Art Center. Designed primarily as an educational resource, ArtsConnectEd is ideal for casual browsing, serious research, and curriculum development. The generous support of the State of Minnesota and MCI WorldCom makes ArtsConnectEd possible.
