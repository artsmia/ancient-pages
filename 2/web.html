Web

The Minneapolis Institute of Arts officially launched the museum's website (www.artsmia.org) on January 4th, 1994. Since that time the museum has developed a number of award-winning online educational resources, programs, and online exhibitions. Most recently the Interactive Media Group has begun to develop and integrate hybrid programs, which are available both on the web as well as within the museum's walls.
