Prints & Processes
Galleries 321 & 355 (Show Map)

This program presents 48 works from the Institute's vast collection of European and North American prints. Commentary and visual cues help convey concepts related to art history, aesthetics, and technique. Video and 3-D animations vividly illustrate printmaking processes.
