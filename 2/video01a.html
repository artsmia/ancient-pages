Prints and Processes

How can you tell the difference between a lithograph and a sillkscreen? How are etchings and aquatints done? How is a woodcut made? Watch contemporary printmakers demonstrate relief, intaglio, lithography, and screenprinting processes in clear, easy-to-follow steps as they create prints based on the same simple image of a tree.
